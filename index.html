Biography

Jiahui Hu is currently a Ph.D candidate with Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Zhibo Wang. She received the M.S. degree in Cyber Security from Wuhan University, China, in 2019. She received the B.S. degree in Information Security from Wuhan University, China, in 2016. Her research interest focuses on federated learning and privacy.


Research Interests

AI Security, Privacy and Security in Federated Learning, Mobile Crowdsensing.
